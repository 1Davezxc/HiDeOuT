About Our Guild

We started our journey way back in GrudgeMU, moved through PrideMU, and eventually made our home in ZhyperMU, where we continue to grow stronger as both players and friends.

Over the years, we’ve faced countless battles, bosses, and wars, but what truly kept us going is the bond we’ve built with each other.

For us, MU Online isn’t just a game – it’s a place where friendships turned into brotherhood. We’ve celebrated victories, endured defeats, and supported each other both inside and outside the game.
